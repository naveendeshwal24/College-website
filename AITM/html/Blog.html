Online Certificatin & Programs

Advanced Institute of Technology and Management (AITM), established in 2006 is the first engineering college in Palwal District. The institute is approved by AICTE and DTE and affiliated to J.C. Bose University of Science & Technology, YMCA, Faridabad. AITM offers courses like BTech, BBA, BCA, MTech, MBA and MCA at the undergraduate and the postgraduate level.Moreover, the institution has modern infrastructure with well-equipped laboratories having sophisticated instruments and highly experienced and dedicated faculty members. The training and placement cell of AITM has tie ups with numerous management firms. The highest salary package that a student gets on completion of courses from AITM is around INR 7.2 LPA.
